Summer

Olaf loves summer because it represents warmth, joy, and fun, all of which resonate with his cheerful personality. He is intrigued by the concept of heat and sun, despite being a snowman, showing his adventurous spirit and curiosity.

Winter

He loves winter because it’s his natural habitat, allowing him to exist and play without the fear of melting. Also, the fun winter activities, such as snowball fights and sledding, align with his playful and jovial character.
